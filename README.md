# 📊 Sales Performance Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is a **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze and visualize business sales performance, profitability, and operational efficiency. It helps in understanding key business metrics and making data-driven decisions.

## 🎯 Key Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance
- Track revenue, cost, and profit trends
- Compare actual vs target revenue
- Evaluate product category and region performance
- Understand sales channel contribution (Online vs Offline)

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Revenue  
- Total Cost  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin %  
- Total Units Sold  
- Revenue per Unit  
- Channel-wise Revenue Contribution  


## 📈 Visualizations Used
- 📊 Revenue by Region (Column Chart)
- 📦 Product Category Performance (Bar Chart)
- 📈 Monthly Revenue Trend (Line Chart)
- 🍩 Sales Channel Distribution (Donut Chart)
- 📊 Target vs Actual Revenue (Clustered Column Chart)
- 📋 Region Performance Matrix (Revenue, Profit, Achievement %)
  
## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Data Visualization

## 📌 Key Insights
- Identified top-performing regions and product categories  
- Compared actual performance against business targets  
- Analyzed revenue contribution across sales channels  
- Evaluated profitability and cost efficiency  

## 🚀 Business Impact
This dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Improve sales strategy
- Optimize product performance
- Monitor financial health
- Track goal achievement efficiently
